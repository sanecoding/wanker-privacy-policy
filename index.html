Privacy Policy for Solo Streak

Effective Date: July 21, 2026
Last Updated: July 21, 2026

Ikitai Apps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application, Solo Streak, collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the app.

1. Information Collection and Storage

Solo Streak is designed with privacy as a priority.

  • Local Device Storage: We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, IP address, or location) on external servers.
  • On-Device Data: All application data—including timer duration, active countdown state, and selected motivational quotes—is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is erased if you uninstall or clear the app data.

2. Device Permissions and Services

To function properly, Solo Streak utilizes the following device permissions and background services:

  • Foreground Service & System Notifications: The app uses a background foreground service (flutter_foreground_task) solely to display a persistent notification showing your active countdown timer when the app is minimized.
  • Audio Playback: The app accesses device media services strictly to play the local alarm audio file (alarm.mp3) when a timer reaches zero.
  • Notification Permissions: We request permission to send local notifications so you receive completion alerts when your timer finishes.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Solo Streak may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies or device advertising identifiers to serve ads based on non-personally identifiable usage patterns.

  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our application and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You can manage or opt out of personalized advertising in your device’s Google Settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
  • For more information on how Google handles data, please visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

4. Children’s Privacy

Solo Streak does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted by the app, no children’s data is stored or shared.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any updates.
